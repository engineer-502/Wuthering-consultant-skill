# Resonator Benchmarks — Functional Seed

These are practical consulting heuristics, not simulator claims. Always verify character-specific targets with current guides for precision advice.

## Universal investment checks

1. Resonator level and weapon level usually beat low-quality echo min-maxing early.
2. For damage dealers, correct Echo main stats and Sonata set matter before chasing perfect substats.
3. For support/sub-DPS, Concerto generation, Outro value, Energy Regen, and buff uptime can beat personal DPS.
4. For sustain, healing/shield consistency and rotation time are more important than screenshot peak stats.

## Common stat bands

| Role | Practical targets to verify per resonator |
|---|---|
| Crit DPS | CRIT Rate/DMG balance, attribute DMG, ATK or scaling stat, enough ER for Liberation cycle |
| Liberation DPS | Higher ER if Liberation is required every rotation; avoid sacrificing all CRIT for ER |
| Basic/Heavy Attack DPS | Check Basic/Heavy DMG bonuses, Outro buffs, active Echo timing, animation/stack rules |
| Echo Skill DPS | Active Echo cooldown/damage window, Echo Skill DMG bonuses, Sonata synergy |
| Concerto support | ER + Concerto efficiency, fast swap routes, buff Outro target correctness |
| Healer/shielder | Healing Bonus/HP/DEF/scaling stat, ER, sustain uptime, team utility |

## Echo cost defaults

- Common DPS baseline: `4-3-3-1-1` with CRIT 4-cost, two 3-cost attribute/ATK/ER as appropriate, and two 1-cost ATK/scaling stats.
- Supports may prefer ER or utility main stats when they unlock reliable Liberation/Outro cycles.
- Never force a 4-cost CRIT Echo if the character-specific kit or current guide favors a special active Echo/stat plan.

## Mechanics checklist

- Does the team feed the intended Outro into the intended on-field carry?
- Is Concerto ready at the planned swap point?
- Does Liberation happen every required cycle, or is ER too low?
- Is the active Echo used inside the correct buff/debuff window?
- Are Tune Break opportunities relevant to this encounter and team?
- Are supports stealing field time from the carry?
